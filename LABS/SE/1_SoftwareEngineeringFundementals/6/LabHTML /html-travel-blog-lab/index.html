Fire Island National Seashore: New York’s Natural Haven

March 07, 2026 Blog Post

Fire Island offers a car-free, relaxing escape just a short ferry ride from Long Island, featuring pristine, uncrowded beaches, the unique Sunken Forest, and charming, walkable communities like Ocean Beach and the Pines. Key highlights include vibrant nightlife, bicycling, and natural beauty.

My trip was a perfect mix of tranquil beach days and lively evenings. The car-free environment, where the tallest building is no higher than the trees, immediately helped me unwind.

Days were spent walking the vast shoreline, browsing shops, and visiting the unique Sunken Forest. Evenings brought fantastic dining with ocean views, followed by dancing and drag shows at the Ice Palace in Cherry Grove.
